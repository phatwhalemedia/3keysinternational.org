3Keys International is a non-profit organization. Our primary objective is to provide individuals with the necessary tools to enable them to overcome any personal obstacles that prevent them from reaching their full potential in their personal and professional lives.

To promote deep emotional healing utilizing the 3Keys model, through training and certification adhering to the highest ethical standards for all 3Keys Practitioners. To provide clients with the necessary tools to identify and resolve subconscious programming and to regain their ability to make free, clear, and rational choices for their own good.

PAT
WYMAN

Pat Wyman has a Masters in Education-Counseling from the University of Missouri St. Louis, is a Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI®) Master Practitioner and has been an Inner-Child Therapist for 30 years. She is trained in the Enneagram, imaging and Neurolinguistic Programming. Pat has also served as consultant to various business and ministry groups, and has presented workshops for corporate teams, small companies, ministry groups, faculties and retreats. She is consultant to Safaa for Educational Consulting, Saudi Arabia. She has presented workshops at the International Enneagram Conference, a variety of MBTI® regional and international conferences including the MBTI® Clinical Conference and the National Wellness Conference. She was the keynote speaker for the British Association of Psychological Type 2009 conference. Pat has published articles in the Enneagram Monthly, Dream Network Journal, Bulletin of Psychological Type and the Journal of Psychological Type. She has been an adjunct faculty member of the Center for Application of Psychological Type (CAPT).

In her workshops, Pat facilitates and guides participants in identifying and addressing issues that prevent a person, a relationship or a team from working at optimum performance. With instruction and experiential exercises, participants learn how to recognize the impediments to living authentically and how to maximize abilities, strengths and assets.

Wyman believes that understanding and appreciating the variety of talents of different personality types enables a person to fully utilize his/her gifts and abilities and to value the contribution of others.

WYMAN'S
RESEARCH

Wyman's research states that, in varying degrees we all grow up in dysfunctional families, which cannot help but inflict emotional wounds of one sort or another. This results in the development of a defense system and the fragmenting of the Core Self. Only by healing childhood wounds and dismantling the defense system can an individual gain access to his/her Core Self.

By identifying the triggers that move you away from your Core Self, you begin to re-establish access to your inner wisdom, integrating body, mind and soul - thus creating an empowering space to be you.

The 3Keys model demonstrates how understanding the roles of the MBTI® and the Enneagran typing systems facilitates personal growth. Three Keys to Self-Understanding represents the culmination of more than 20 years of Pat Wyman's work. It skillfully balances theoretical and practical information, weaving experiential exercises and techniques into a detailed presentation of real-life cases.
