What are 3Keys Workshops?

A 3Keys workshop is the best way to get to know the 3Keys model. There, you can interact with seasoned Certified Practitioners and newcomers alike, learn and discuss the benefits of the 3Keys approach and how it can help you reach your full potential in your personal and professional life.

3Keys workshops allow attendees to explore the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator® assessment and the Enneagram. This allows newcomers to better understand their personality characteristics, which enables a better understanding of the 3Keys method as applied to each individual.

Online Support

Due to the COVID-19 public health crisis, we are currently suspending face-to-face workshops and offering these online, via Zoom. Each workshop provides you with key tools to help you understand your inner landscape, and how the stress and anxiety caused by the coronavirus pandemic may be affecting you at a deeper level, as well as how you may address this.
